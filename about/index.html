About

Hi! I am Federico and this is my little website, where I try to merge various activities otherwise dispersed all around the web. I am a post-doc researcher at Instituto Complutense de Ciencias Musicales - ICCMU in the Didone project, where I work on the expression of emotions in the 18th century Italian Opera.

I have taken my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Milan in the LIM - Music Informatics Laboratory, where I studied computational methods for music performance analysis. While I am continuously involved in researching new tools for music/audio analysis and synthesis, I want to contribute to the improvement of the society and of the world in which we live.

  • Mail: fsimonetta [at] iccmu.es
  • Main Research interests: music information processing, machine learning, music performance analysis and reconstruction
  • Secondary Research interests: audio processing, medical acoustics, symbolic music analysis and generation, audio synthesis
